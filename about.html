Our Mission
Why We Built SmartFeed

SmartFeed was created to make food access data actionable. Millions of Americans live in neighborhoods classified by the USDA as low-income and low-access areas — places where supermarkets and fresh food are hard to reach. We built SmartFeed to help people understand their food environment, find healthier options nearby, and make informed nutritional choices — all powered by real government data and AI.
